How we work.

We figure out exactly what is broken before we start building anything. This saves time and prevents us from creating tools you do not actually need.

Phase 01

UX Discovery and Research

Everything begins with understanding your current setup. We look at your existing systems to find the actual pain points.

We map out your workflows and identify where your team gets stuck. Sometimes the problem is a confusing database layout. Other times developers cannot find the right integration guides.

Once we have a clear picture of the friction points, we decide on the best solution. From here, our process splits into two possible directions based on what your team needs to succeed. We can also handle both areas if your project requires it.

Phase 02

Execution and Build

We take the research from the discovery phase and start creating the solution. We focus on these two main service areas.

Software Development

If you need a custom tool or a better way to organize data, we build it. First, we create an initial working application using Python or Rust.

Then, our engineering specialist reviews the code to check the logic and optimize performance before it goes live.

  • Custom application creation
  • Dashboard consolidation
  • Workflow automation setup
  • Script optimization

Technical Documentation

If your developers are struggling to adopt your platform, we focus on documentation. We build GitBook portals and create technical diagrams in Figma.

We also write clear onboarding guides to give your team the exact information they need to integrate quickly.

  • GitBook portal setup
  • Figma data flow diagrams
  • New developer onboarding guides
  • Integration walkthroughs
Phase 03

Final Handoff

Once the work is done, we hand everything over to you. We make sure your team knows exactly how to use the new setup.

You get full ownership of the custom code and the final documentation. We provide deployment instructions and a maintenance guide so your team can easily update things in the future.
